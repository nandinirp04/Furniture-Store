About the Owner

Mukhtar Khan, the founder of HK Furniture, has always been passionate about design and craftsmanship. With over a decade of experience in the furniture industry, he launched HK Furniture in 2022 with a mission to provide high-quality, stylish, and affordable furniture to the people of Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar.

To Mukhtar, furniture is more than just a functional item; it’s what transforms a space. He believes in creating pieces that offer both comfort and beauty, ensuring each item is built to last and meets modern trends.

His commitment to quality, attention to detail, and personalized customer service have been the driving forces behind the store’s success. Whether you're furnishing your home or office, Mukhtar and his team are dedicated to helping you find the perfect pieces that make your space feel like home.
